Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. Previously, I served as an Assistant Professor at Dayananda Sagar University. My research lies at the intersection of Computational Fluid Mechanics (CFD) and Artificial Intelligence. I specialize in investigating the dynamic behavior of fluids—specifically linear and non-linear instability—using advanced numerical methods.

Beyond the equations, I am a dedicated athlete and a motivational speaker, believing strongly in the synergy between physical discipline and academic excellence.
