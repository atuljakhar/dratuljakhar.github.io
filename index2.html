About Me

Hello! I'm Dr. Atul Jakhar, currently working as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. My work sits at the intersection of classical mechanics and modern technology, specifically exploring Computational Fluid Mechanics (CFD) and the application of Artificial Intelligence in fluid dynamics.


My research investigates the dynamic behavior of fluids through numerical methods and simulations, with a particular focus on instability and chaos. Beyond academia, I am a passionate athlete and motivational speaker, believing that the discipline required in sports translates directly to scientific rigor.
